Roof Leaks

Storm Damage

Missing shingles, lifted edges, punctures, and impact damage—repairs performed with durable materials and details.

Flashing Failures

Chimneys, walls, valleys, and edges—flashing details are rebuilt to prevent recurring leaks.

Vent & Pipe Seals

Damaged Shingles

Cracks, blow-offs, granule loss, and curling—replaced to restore roof integrity and appearance.

Drainage Issues

Ponding water and clogged drainage create leaks—solutions focus on flow, seals, and roof detail integrity.
